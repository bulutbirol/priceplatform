# Computer Hardware and Accessories Expansion

## Scope

Expand Personal Technology with sixteen leaf categories while preserving the three-level browser.

## Families

- Computer Systems: laptop, desktop, mini PC, all-in-one computer.
- Computer Hardware: processor, graphics card, motherboard, RAM, SSD, power supply, computer case, processor cooler.
- Peripherals: monitor, keyboard, mouse, webcam, printer.
- Storage and Connectivity: external storage and docking station.
- Existing mobile, photography, audio, and gaming families remain.

## Content

Every new leaf receives twelve product-specific technologies, twelve matching product-specific price factors, three guides, a four-system visual map, detailed trade-offs, and a buying checklist.

## Accuracy

Profiles are authored separately for physical components. A graphics card may include VRAM and cooling, while RAM may include memory chips and timings; shared group terms are not copied blindly.

## Validation

The tree must contain 88 unique products. Tests verify representative hardware and accessory paths and require each new category to satisfy the leaf content contract.
